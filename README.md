# 🛒 Quantium Retail Analytics Case Study

> Retail Analytics using Python | Quantium Data Analytics Virtual Experience (Forage)

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project was completed as part of the **Quantium Data Analytics Virtual Experience Program on Forage**.

The objective was to analyze retail transaction and customer purchase data to understand customer purchasing behaviour and provide strategic recommendations for the Category Manager responsible for the Chips category.

The analysis follows a real-world analytics workflow, including data cleaning, feature engineering, exploratory data analysis, customer segmentation, and business insights.

---

## 🎯 Business Problem

The Category Manager wanted to answer questions such as:

- Which customer segments purchase the most chips?
- Which segments contribute the highest sales?
- Which brands and pack sizes are most popular?
- What factors drive chip sales?
- Which customer segments should be targeted?

The final objective was to provide data-driven recommendations for the upcoming category review.

## ⚙️ Feature Engineering

Additional variables were created from the product descriptions:

- Brand Name
- Pack Size

These features were later used to analyze purchasing behaviour across different customer segments.

---

## 📈 Key Metrics Analysed

The analysis focused on:

- Total Sales
- Number of Customers
- Number of Transactions
- Average Spend per Customer
- Average Price per Unit
- Purchase Frequency
- Units Purchased per Transaction
- Sales by Customer Segment
- Sales by Brand
- Sales by Pack Size

---

## 📊 Key Insights

Some important findings from the analysis include:

- Customer purchasing behaviour differs significantly across life stages.
- Total sales alone are not sufficient to evaluate customer segments.
- Purchase frequency and average spend provide better insight into customer value.
- Brand preference and pack size vary across customer segments.

---

## 💼 Business Recommendation

Based on the analysis, recommendations were made to help the Category Manager:

- Identify high-value customer segments.
- Focus marketing efforts on profitable customer groups.
- Optimize product assortment based on popular brands and pack sizes.
- Support future category planning using data-driven insights.
